About Emily

Emily is a former middle school teacher and Teach For America alumna with experience creating culturally relevant social studies and writing curricula in Houston and New York City. She currently works as a Program Manager for Code Nation, a tech-ed nonprofit that equips students from under-resourced high schools with the skills, experiences, and connections that together create access to careers in technology. Emily loves going on adventures around and out of the city, reading voraciously, and learning new things.
